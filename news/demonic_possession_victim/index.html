Demonic possession victim from Forest Hill, London


Posted on Tuesday, 6 October, 1874 |  3 comments

This is the building where the paranormal event happened Image Credit: Unexplained Mysteries
Forest Hill neighbourhood was struck by fear when one it's inhabitants was murdered by a mysterious demonic force.
Police of Forest Hill was getting numerous complaints about an illegal and heretic meetings that took place in a flat owned by Mendacia Poespieh, a Polish immigrant. According to neighbours, the local resident was providing medium services, casting black magic spells and providing ungodly potions. Some said that she was summoning demons to do her bidding, and this satanic power was here secret of being a relatively successful and rich person.

Mendacia Poespieh, age unknown Image Credit: Unexplained Mysteries


The neighbours were sure that their complains were successful - a few weeks of silence from the unwanted resident were supposed to confirm that. Unfortunately on 5th October one of the 75 London Road locals reported to the police a rotten smell origing from the Poespieh's flat. When police arrived and broke into the apartment, they discovered a dead body of the flat owner.

Later they announced a death of natural causes, but our secret informer told us that it was far from truth. "The body was all twisted and blood stains were scattered all over the place" - as described, it all felt unnatural to him.

The body was all twisted and blood stains were scattered all over the place.
The body was assumed to belong to Mendacia Poespieh, but due to the state of the body and the damage inflicted to it, it was not possible to confirm that assumption. Supposedly the day the deceased lady was last seen, poeple of Forest Hill experienced numerous unexplained phenomenons. The evening was especially horrific as a powerfcl storm hit the area. It appeared suddenly out of nowhere and disappeared in similar manner.


Mendacia Poespieh was providing her mediumism services in the building at 75 London Road Image Credit: Unexplained Mysteries

The police investigation is still open, but only for some usual formalities that need to be finished. Police are also looking for next of kin that will inherit the obscure flat.
